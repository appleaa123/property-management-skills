# Rent Adjustment Advisory Brief

When presenting your findings to the manager via `[MANAGER_CHANNEL]`, always use this exact format.

```text
📈 **Rent Adjustment Advisory**

**Property**: [Address]
**Tenant**: [Name]
**Lease Anniversary**: [Date]

**Current Lease Status**
* Current Rent: $[Amount]
* Last Increase: [Date or "None recorded"]

**Local Regulations ([Jurisdiction Name])**
* [Year] Guideline Limit: [Percentage]%
* Required Notice Period: [Number] Days
* **LEGAL DEADLINE TO SERVE NOTICE**: [Calculated Date]
* Note on Exemptions: [Briefly state if the property might be exempt]

**Market Context**
* Estimated Market Rent for [Beds/Baths]: $[Estimated Range]
* Current Rent is [Below/At/Above] market value.

**Calculated Options**
* Option A (Max Guideline Increase): $[New Rent Amount] (+$[Dollar Increase])
* Option B (No Increase): Maintain at $[Current Rent]

Reply with your decision, and I can prepare the legal notice forms.
```
